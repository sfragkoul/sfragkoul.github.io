## Overview

This work was conducted within the [ELIXIR Machine Learning Focus Group](https://elixir-europe.org/focus-groups/machine-learning) and focuses on one of the major open challenges in synthetic data research: how to systematically evaluate the quality, reliability and utility of synthetic datasets in life sciences.
Following PRISMA guidelines, we systematically reviewed more than 8000 records from multiple scientific databases and curated 188 publications to identify currently used synthetic data evaluation metrics across domains.


## Related Publication

Paper was published in *NAR Genomics and Bioinformatics* and can be found [here](https://doi.org/10.1093/nargab/lqag012).
